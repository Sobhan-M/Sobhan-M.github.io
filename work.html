Freelance Content Writer

May, 2020 - Current

After the COVID-19 pandemic took away my other sources of income, I decided to make the best of the situation and try to make an income online. Writing was the one skill I thought I would do best at, as I had been paid for my writing a few times in the past.

Description

My clients send the topic they want written, as well as the word count they want. My job is to research the topic to find useful information, and write the articles, blogs, or copies my clients want.

I'm happy to report that my clients are more than happy with my work, and I've continuously tried to improve my craft by taking reputable seminars.

Freelance Photographer

September, 2019 - March, 2020

I started doing freelance photography after I moved to Montreal and started University. I used my hobby to make a bit of income to support my studies until I could find a more reliable form of employment. Unfortunately, finding even basic work without speaking French in Montreal proved much harder than I imagined.

That's why my temporary means of income lasted for longer than I would have expected, before coming to an end because of the COVID-19 lockdowns.
