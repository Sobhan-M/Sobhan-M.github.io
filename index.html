What Is This Website?

This website is a complete, dynamic, and easy to use online resume. It's here to present all the information about me in a neat little package. Thus allowing you to make a better assessment regarding my skills and abilities. It also gives you more context into who I am as an individual beyond what I can put down on a piece of paper.

What Can You Expect To Find Here?

You can expect to see a clear indication of my skills, as I'll be highlighting and presenting all my abilities within this website. You'll also be able to take a look at all the projects I've worked on, even if some are irrelevant to the position I'm applying for. Finally, you can expect to get an honest representation of who I am as a person, so you can reach your own conclusion about me.

Who Made This Website?

I made this website entirely on my own, using basic HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that I had learnt from university. I did consult web resources while making this, as some elements/features were foreign to me. My primary web sources were fireship.io and w3schools.com. The icons you see are directly from fontawesome.com, and I'm using the Cabin font which is imported using the Google API.
